Our Story

Moyaliso Meat Supplier is built on the foundation of unwavering quality. We source our livestock from trusted Kenyan farms, ensuring ethical treatment and superior meat products. Our commitment is to deliver freshness that you can taste.

From prime cuts of Goat and Mutton to specialized Dormer meat and traditional offals, every product is handled with the utmost care, guaranteeing hygienic standards and perfect preparation.
