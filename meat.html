Our Premium Meat Offerings

Explore the quality cuts and traditional offals sourced from our trusted farms.

Goat Meat

Animal Source: Indigenous Kenyan Goat (Mbuzi)

Goat meat is lean, finely textured, and prized for its rich, slightly sweet flavor. We offer prime cuts including leg, shoulder, loin chops, and cubes, perfect for traditional stews (like 'nyama choma') and grilling.

Available as whole carcass or custom breakdown for wholesale and retail.

Mutton / Sheep Meat

Animal Source: Local Sheep (Kondoo)

Mutton offers a deeper, more mature flavor than goat, ideal for slow-cooked dishes and rich curries. Our selection includes fatty cuts like ribs and breast, as well as lean legs and racks.

All meat is aged slightly to enhance tenderness and flavor profile.

Dormer Meat

Animal Source: Dormer Breed Sheep

Dormer sheep are known for yielding a superior quality, lean carcass with excellent meat-to-bone ratio. This meat is highly favored for high-end dining due to its tenderness and mild flavor. Cuts include premium loins and fillets.

This is our premium option, ensuring consistent quality and taste.

Liver

Source: Goat and Sheep

Our liver is fresh, nutrient-dense, and carefully prepared immediately after slaughter. It is available whole or sliced, perfect for quick frying or inclusion in traditional meat platters.

We ensure minimal cooling time to maintain peak freshness.

Intestines & Stomach (Offals)

Source: Goat and Sheep

These traditional offals are handled hygienically, meticulously cleaned, and ready for use in local delicacies. They are an essential part of the Kenyan traditional cooking and are supplied fresh or chilled.

Ideal for slow-cooking and preparing rich, flavorful broth.
